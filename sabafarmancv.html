FINAL YEAR PROJECT

UAV Based Land Cover Analysis Using Remote Sensing


The aim of this project is to transmit a live stream from the field to a designated system. This project uses a UAV for such surveys that can obtain high resolution images and videos and transmit them to server in real time with integration of Raspberry Pi. The footage transmitted must be high resolution having low latency and georeferenced in real time .These UAVs do not require a team to operate them and hence the amount of manpower and cost is minimized substantially. UAVs can cover large areas much faster than humans and can gather data in a matter of minutes which would otherwise take hours to collect. Target is to reduce the cost of conducting surveys, save time and increase productivity.
